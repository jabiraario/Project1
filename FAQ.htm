Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM every day. Our team is ready to serve you delicious coffee and treats all day!

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a selection of vegan and gluten-free options available. Just ask our barista or check the labels on our menu.

Do you have free Wi-Fi?

Absolutely! Our coffee shop offers free Wi-Fi so you can work, study, or simply enjoy a coffee break with online access.

Can I reserve a table for a group?

Yes, we accept reservations for groups of up to 10 people. Please call us at least one day in advance to reserve a spot.
